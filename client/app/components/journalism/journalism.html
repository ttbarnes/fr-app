Journalism by Fiona

As well as working as a Jazz artist, Fiona spends her time behind the scenes in Jazz and is a member of the Jazz Journalists Association, The British Association of Journalists, and womeninjournalism.co.uk

She is the founder of  Women in Jazz Media - a trailblazing organisation created to support and encourage a more diverse Jazz community.

Fiona is also a senior writer for Jazz in Europe with her own Women in Jazz series and currently the guest Editor in Chief for Jazz Quarterly.
