Journalism by Fiona

As well as working as a Jazz artist, Fiona spends her time working as a Jazz journalist and supporting a number of organisations that actively support equality, diversity and inclusion. She is member of the Jazz Journalists Association, The British Association of Journalists, a senior writer for Jazz in Europe, with her own Women in Jazz series, as well as working for Jazz Quarterly and The Jazz UK. Interview highlights include Steve Gadd, Dee Dee Bridgewater, Maxine Gordon, Michel Camilo, Terri Lyne Carrington and Kyle Eastwood.

For reviews or interview requests please contact info@fionaross.co.uk
