Journalism by Fiona

As well as working as a Jazz artist, Fiona spends her time as a writer and supporting a number of companies and organisations. She is a senior writer with her own Women In Jazz series for Jazz in Europe. Fiona is also a creative writer for Jazz Quarterly and writes for Jazz Connects and Pearson. Fiona is also a member of the Jazz Journalists Association and The British Association of Journalists.
